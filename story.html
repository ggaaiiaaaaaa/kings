Our Story

A legacy of empowering the hardworking Filipino since 1999.

Our Vision

To be the leading professional workforce provider with satisfied clients and secured members in the Philippines.

Our Mission

Driven with passion, agility, and excellence in everything we do, we are committed to maintain a successful partnership with our clients as we also help with the needs and aspirations of our members.

Staying true to our purpose, it is our mission to be a responsible member of the Cooperative Development Authority in contributing to the growth of the economy by continuously providing jobs.

Our Core Values

Member-Centric

Our members’ welfare is our top priority. We help improve the lives of our members by providing jobs that uplift their dignity.

Truth

We strive to promote and implement truthful and transparent transactions with our Clients and Co-Members.

Quality

What we do, we do well. Delivering only the best products and exceptional services for you.

Value

Developing systems to ensure the best possible return on investment for our members, clients and other stakeholders.

Integrity

Possessing and practicing fairness and objectivity and strong moral principles even in the most challenging situations.

Excellence

Providing our clients with superb services and exceptional overall experience, exceeding expectations by working smart and hard.

Innovation

Actively creative and open to all possibilities in meeting our clients' unique needs.

Accountability

Demonstrating dependability and personal ownership necessary in achieving desired results.

Professionalism

Making sure that everyone who seeks our services is treated with respect, courtesy and efficiency.

Our Origin

Since 1999, Kings has been redefining the staffing industry.

1999

KINGS Lending Corporation

Founded in Zamboanga City providing microfinancing services.

2009

KINGS Manpower

Established to provide manpower services to different industries in Zamboanga City.

2011

Metro Manila Expansion

Expanded footprint to the capital, establishing KINGS Headquarters with offices in Parañaque, Manila, Makati, and BGC.

2016

Visayas Expansion

KINGS Manpower expands footprint further into the Visayas region.

2017

KINGS Group of Companies

Consolidated our diverse entities into the unified Kings Group of Companies.

2019

10,000 Members & KINGS City

Reached the 10,000 member milestone. Expanded to serviced offices, coworking space, and KINGS City.

2020

Home Culinary & Technical School

Established a TESDA-accredited training center to offer livelihood and educational programs directly to KINGS Members.
