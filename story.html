The Cooperative Difference

Pioneering an ethical global staffing model that rewards the talent driving your success.

The traditional BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) model is broken. It's built on a foundation of extracting maximum value from local talent while providing minimum stability and reward in return. This leads to burnout, high attrition, and ultimately, a breakdown in service quality for international clients.

At Kings Group, we saw an opportunity to redefine the relationship between global businesses and Philippine talent. We decided to build a model rooted in ownership.

"We aren't just staff. We are partners in your success, because we have a literal stake in our own."

The Worker-Owned Advantage

Kings Group operates as a worker-owned cooperative. When you hire through us, you aren't paying a traditional agency markup that funnels out to external shareholders. Your capital directly supports the wealth and development of your team.

Our members enjoy profit-sharing, premium health benefits, career coaching, and access to internal financial tools like Kings Lending to support their major life milestones.
